Research field: Algebraic geometry

Research interest: Birational geometry and its application, e.g. MMP (Minimal Model Program), the theory of complements, boundedness of varieties, the ACC conjecture for minimal log discrepancies, foliations, and K-stability (the existence of K\"{a}hler-Einstein metrics). Symbolic computation, e.g. CAD (Cylindrical Algebraic Decomposition) and automated polynomial inequalities proving.
